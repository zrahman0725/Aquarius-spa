About Us

A Brand New Experience!

Aquarius Spa is your destination where relaxation meets rejuvenation under the care of our new management. We have enhanced our offerings to provide you with an unparalleled sauna experience, featuring a dry sauna, steam room, cold shower bucket, massage chair, lounge room, karaoke, kids zone, and a cozy fireplace. Enjoy a welcoming and homey atmosphere with plenty of table games to unwind and socialize. Whether you call it a sauna, steam room, spa, baths, or banya, our authentic Eastern European sauna experience is the perfect escape to de-stress and revitalize. Discover more about the benefits of our services and how they can enhance your well-being by exploring our website. Join us at Aquarius Spa and embark on a journey to holistic relaxation and wellness.
